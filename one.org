* The Horizon: Your First Clue the Earth is Round
:PROPERTIES:
:ONE: one-default
:CUSTOM_ID: /debunking-horizon/
:END:

** The vanishing point illusion vs. actual curvature

Flat earthers claim that objects disappear at the horizon due to
"perspective" — that is, they simply get too small to see. This is
demonstrably false and easy to test.

** The experiment anyone can run

Take a camera with a zoom lens to the ocean. Watch a ship sail away.
Notice that the *hull disappears before the mast*. This is not
perspective — zooming in restores the full size of the ship's upper
structure, but the bottom remains hidden.

This happens because the Earth curves *downward away from you* at a
rate of roughly 8 inches per mile squared. At 10 miles distance, the
surface has dropped approximately 66 feet below your line of sight.

** The Bedford Level Experiment

In 1838, Samuel Rowbotham conducted the famous Bedford Level experiment,
claiming to show the Earth was flat. In 1870, John Hampden's wager led
Alfred Russel Wallace to repeat it *correctly* — using proper controls
for atmospheric refraction — conclusively proving curvature.

Modern laser experiments and high-resolution cameras have replicated this
hundreds of times. The curvature is measurable and consistent with a
spherical Earth of radius ~6,371 km.

** The math

For an observer at height ~h~ meters, the horizon distance ~d~ in
kilometers is:

~d ≈ √(2Rh)~

where R = 6,371 km. For a person 1.7m tall, the horizon is ~4.7 km
away. This formula has been verified by sailors, surveyors, and
aircraft pilots for centuries.

* Gravity: Why a Flat Earth Can't Hold Its Own Atmosphere
:PROPERTIES:
:ONE: one-default
:CUSTOM_ID: /debunking-gravity/
:END:

** The atmosphere problem

A flat disc cannot gravitationally retain an atmosphere. On a sphere,
gravity points toward the center of mass uniformly from all surface
points. On a flat disc, gravity would point *downward through the disc*
— meaning the atmosphere at the edges would drift off into space.

Flat earthers often claim the disc accelerates upward at 9.8 m/s² to
simulate gravity. This requires:

1. An infinite energy source
2. Relativistic speeds within ~1 year of continuous acceleration
3. An explanation of why gravity varies with latitude and altitude

None of these have been provided.

** Gravitational variation

Gravity is not uniform across Earth's surface. It is slightly stronger
at the poles (~9.832 m/s²) than at the equator (~9.780 m/s²). This is
*precisely* what a rotating oblate spheroid predicts — the equatorial
bulge places you slightly farther from the center of mass, and
centrifugal force reduces the effective gravitational pull.

This variation has been measured to extraordinary precision by
gravimeters and is used daily in oil exploration, geology, and civil
engineering. It has no explanation in any flat earth model.

** Tides

Tides are caused by the differential gravitational pull of the Moon and
Sun across Earth's diameter. They follow predictable cycles governed by
orbital mechanics — cycles that have been calculated centuries in advance
with perfect accuracy.

On a flat earth, there is no coherent explanation for why two tidal
bulges occur simultaneously on opposite sides of the Earth.

* Antarctica: Circumnavigation and the Impossible Ice Wall
:PROPERTIES:
:ONE: one-default
:CUSTOM_ID: /debunking-antarctica/
:END:

** The flat earth map's fatal flaw

The most popular flat earth model places Antarctica as a giant ice wall
surrounding the disc. This makes a testable prediction: the southern
hemisphere should be *much larger* than the northern hemisphere. Flights
and sea routes should take radically longer when traveling through the
south.

They don't.

** Direct flights and sailing routes

A flight from Sydney to Johannesburg takes approximately 11 hours and
travels roughly southwest. On the flat earth map, this route would be
thousands of kilometers longer than it actually is — the flight would
need to curve far north via Asia and Africa.

Multiple circumnavigations of Antarctica have been completed by ship
and aircraft. The distance is consistently ~22,000 km — exactly what
you'd expect for a continent of Antarctica's actual size at ~70°S
latitude on a sphere. On the flat earth model, this journey would be
~60,000+ km.

** Antarctica Treaty and the "conspiracy"

Flat earthers claim a global conspiracy keeps people from visiting the
ice wall. In reality, Antarctica hosts ~70 permanent research stations
from 30+ countries. Thousands of tourists, scientists, and adventurers
visit annually. Dozens of private expeditions have circumnavigated the
continent.

No credible conspiracy explanation accounts for the coordinated silence
of scientists from the US, Russia, China, and dozens of other nations
— many of which are fierce geopolitical rivals.

* The ISS, Satellites, and Live Video from Space

:ONE: one-default

** You can see the ISS with your own eyes

The International Space Station is visible to the naked eye from any
location on Earth. Websites like NASA's Spot the Station provide exact
times and sky positions — and they are accurate to the second.

If the ISS were a fake projection or high-altitude plane, its trajectory
would not follow the precise orbital mechanics predicted by Kepler's
laws. Amateur astronomers photograph it nightly with telescopes,
capturing detail of its solar panels.

** Satellites and GPS

The Global Positioning System consists of 31+ operational satellites
in medium Earth orbit at ~20,200 km altitude. Your phone uses
triangulation from multiple satellites simultaneously to determine
your position to within a few meters.

This only works if:
- The satellites exist
- They are where orbital mechanics predicts they will be
- The speed-of-light delays match the distances involved

Flat earth models cannot account for GPS accuracy, nor for the
existence of geostationary satellites (which must be directly above
the equator and orbit at ~35,786 km to remain stationary relative
to Earth's surface).

** Live footage

Multiple space agencies — NASA, ESA, JAXA, Roscosmos — as well as
private companies like SpaceX broadcast live, unedited footage from
space. Independent amateur astronomers point telescopes at the ISS
and livestream independently. The curvature of Earth is visible in
all footage taken from sufficient altitude.

* Ships Over the Horizon: The Oldest Proof in the Book
:PROPERTIES:
:ONE: one-default
:CUSTOM_ID: /debunking-ships/
:END:

** Aristotle knew

In 350 BCE, Aristotle listed the ships-disappearing-hull-first
observation as evidence that Earth is a sphere. This is among the
oldest surviving empirical arguments for a spherical Earth.

Nothing has changed since then except our ability to zoom in with
cameras and confirm that the hull is genuinely *behind the curvature*
— not merely too small to see.

** The zoom test

When a ship disappears over the horizon hull-first, zooming in with
binoculars or a telephoto lens restores the upper structure of the ship
clearly — the mast, the bridge, the funnel. The hull remains invisible.

If disappearance were due to perspective, zooming in would restore the
entire ship. The hull's absence proves it is geometrically occluded by
the curvature of the Earth.

** Lighthouses

The height of lighthouses is determined by their required visibility
range — which is calculated using the curvature of the Earth. A
lighthouse at 30m height is visible at roughly 19 km. Raise it to
120m and it's visible at ~39 km.

These calculations have been used by maritime engineers for centuries.
Ships have not been running aground in large numbers due to visibility
miscalculation. The formula works because the Earth is a sphere.

The evidence for a spherical Earth is not a matter of trusting

- Directly observable with modest equipment
- Mathematically consistent across independent domains
- Confirmed by rival nations and independent researchers worldwide
- Centuries old, predating all modern governments and space agencies

The Earth is not flat. It has never been flat. The question was settled
